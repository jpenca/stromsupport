Attention

To connect your Rytm Drum Computer via USB, Overbridge must be disabled. To connect wirelessly, get the latest version of SDS Drop from the Mac App Store. Make sure your Mac and your iPad are on the same local network.

On your Rytm machine, enter the GLOBAL menu, then go to SYSTEM > USB CONFIG. Set USB mode to "USB-MIDI ONLY".
