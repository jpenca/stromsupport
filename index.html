Connecting STROM to your Elektron Analog Rytm

USB Connection

To connect via USB, you need a Lightning to USB adapter. On your Rytm, disable Overbridge mode (GLOBAL > SYSTEM > USB CONFIG > USB-MIDI ONLY).

Wireless Connection

To connect STROM wirelessly, you need a Mac running the SDS Drop app.

  • Connect your Rytm to your Mac via USB.
  • Make sure that SDS Drop is running on your Mac, and that your iPad and Mac are both in the same local network.
  • In SDS Drop, click on the STROM button and enable the remote connection from there.
